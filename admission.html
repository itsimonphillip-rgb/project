Admissions & Guidelines

Join our community of innovators and leaders

Our Simple 3-Step Process

We've designed our entry path to be as transparent and supportive as possible for families.

1. Apply Online

Fill out our digital application wizard and upload required documents from any smartphone or computer.

2. Entrance Exam

Shortlisted candidates will receive an SMS and email invite to sit for our standard computer-based aptitude tests.

3. Verification & Offer

After review of scores and documents, successful candidates receive official formal admission letters and portal keys.

Minimum Entry Requirements

  • Junior Secondary (JSS1) Must be at least 10 years old by September of the entry year, with evidence of primary school completion.
  • Senior Secondary (SSS1) Successful completion of the Junior School Certificate Examination (BECE) with passing grades in core subjects.
  • Transfers (JSS2 & SSS2 only) Submission of certified academic transcripts and behavioral reference sheets from the previous accredited secondary school.

Required Documents

You will need to scan or take high-quality photos of these files to attach inside our application wizard:

Passport Photo
Birth Certificate
Last Academic Report
Primary Testimonial
