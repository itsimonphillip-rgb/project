Our Journey

The History of Elysium Academy

How a vision of holistic, practical education transformed into a beacon of academic brilliance over the years.

2016

The Foundation Year

Elysium Academy was established with a singular focus: bridging the gap between abstract textbook theories and real-world practical experience. We opened our doors with just a small block of classrooms and a dedicated team of foundational educators.

2021

Science Laboratory Launch

To elevate our commitment to empirical knowledge, we built and launched our state-of-the-art Applied and Basic Science Laboratories. This allowed students to transition from reading about science to actively running experiments.

2026

The Digital & Tech Leap

Marking our full evolution, we completely integrated creative tech tracks, responsive web development basics, and advanced computer logical labs into our core curriculum, ensuring our graduates are completely future-ready.
